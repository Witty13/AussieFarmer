Aussie Farmer
By Cooper Whitbread
Instructions:
    1. Press "Press here to begin" to start the game.
    2. Enter a username. Entering the same username later with get you back to where you last finished.
    3. The aim is to get as many animals as possible. Collect the chicken egg to get some money. Click on either the egg or the Chicken paddock to do this.
    4. When you have enough money, add another chicken by clicking the "Add Head" button. More Chickens will give you more money.
    5. When you have enough money, add a pig by pressing the "Add Head" button underneath the pig to unlock pigs. Pigs will give you more money than chickens but take longer to produce their items
    6. Keep playing until you have unlocked all the animals.
    7. The game will save automatically every 3 seconds. Leave at any time you want and using your username, you will be able to back to where you finished.
    8. Have fun!
